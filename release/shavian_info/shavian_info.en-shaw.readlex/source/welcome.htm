Wordlist Model Documentation

The Shaw ReadLex lexical model is a wordlist ordered by frequency in the British National Corpus and spelled in the Shavian alphabet. The spellings adopted are those in the Kingsley Read Lexicon published by Shavian.info at https://www.shavian.info/dictionary/, which is ultimately derived from the "rhotic Received Pronunciation" standard used in the Shaw Alphabet edition of Androcles and the Lion (1962).

Shaw ReadLex is intended for use with the Shavian Alphabet keyboards available for Keyman. When installed, it provides these keyboards with a rudimentary predictive text capability that reduces typographic errors and increases speed.
